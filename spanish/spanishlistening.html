Duolingo

Resource Type: App and Website

The app has a new feature called Bots where you can have a conversation with a virtual voice. Also, the Spanish course includes exercises where you have to translate or copy down a phrase spoken in Spanish to you.

SpanishPod101

Resource Type: App and Website

This website allows you to listen to dialogue and then cover the grammar points and vocabulary. It is more on the serious side.

FluentU

Resource Type: App and Website

Has a large variety of videos, TV shows, music videos, and movies that allow you to listen to Spanish in an engaging way and then allows you to practice vocabulary from the videos!

ConjuguemosTube

Resource Type: Website

A series of graded wideos for teachers and students with accompanying worksheet, sorted by level and topic.
